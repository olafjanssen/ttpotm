# The words
in cars we move not in space, we move through space as it moves around us
`lon tomo tawa la, ona li tawa ala lon ma. taso ma li sike e ona.`

collapsing our vast surface into a string of lines, or worse, a collection of destinations
`supa suli li pona tawa linja wan, kin, tawa pini mute`

be in space, reclaim your degrees of freedom, walk
`o awen lon ma, o kama jo e wile tawa, o tawa noka`

and let people wonder, how you got there
`jan li pilin e ni: sina lon ni tan seme?`
