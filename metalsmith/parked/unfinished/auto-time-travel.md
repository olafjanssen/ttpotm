In our `ike` experience, we have to stand by hopelessly when we witness our past. We cannot fix the mistakes we made, the past has already happened, right? On the other hand we can look forward to the future as the realm of opportunities, hope and change. Because this situation feels so natural to us, we must understand it the wrong way. So let us forget this natural feeling and consider the alternative.

Let us first acknowledge that it is strange that we consider that what we have knowledge of (the past) is out of our control, yet that what is completely unknown (the future) would be in our power. Does one not need to have or oversee something to control before you can control it?

Communicating with the past is possible. More specifically, it is possible to communicate with your former self. Your former self has no choice on how to perceive your communication, but your future self can. Your current choices are, therefore, influenced by messages sent from the future. The art of communicating properly with your former self, does not only help your former self, but also your current and future self. In this way, you are co-writing your own life story.

But how can you listen to your future self? Due to clear limits in the physical realm, the message will be coded in all types of forms and symbols that your current self cannot control. There is absolutely no certainty that you will interpret the coded messages in the right manner, either. However, your former self will listen to it in such a way that your current self is sending the message that it has sent.

Therefore, it may appear that you cannot control the future but that is because your message has already changed the past as you experience it.


So, you have the power to change the future, by letting your current self write a message to your former self.

Why not write a message in the form of an epic tale of victory? It will unnoticably improve life in the past, but noticeably in the future. Such a rewrite of future history requires a sending ritual.
