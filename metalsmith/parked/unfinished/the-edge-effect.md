The phenomenon that on the boundary between two ecological systems the most unique and new lifeforms develop is called The Edge Effect. Extra creativity exists if multiple cultures and perspectives come together. But also physical balance that signifies a moment of choice; the transition from one world to another; je have to either choose between two worlds or eternally balance on the edge.

Does it always pay off in creativity of does it also lead to stagnation, maybe or maybe not. Perhaps you should always strife to live on the edge like:

* the beach between sea and land
* the event horizon of a black hole
* finding that part of your job that actually does not fit in
* thunderstorms where warm and cold air meet
* the path of life itself
