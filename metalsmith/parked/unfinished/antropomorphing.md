Only people are people, but not only people have feelings. That is why we often speak of or talk with animals as if they are human. For animals that we consider to be a pest we do this less. In more extreme cases we don't even treat humans as humans, in wars, refugees or strangers on the bus.

Why not treat everything as a close relative or as you want to be treated yourself. Give your bread a soul that you can treat well but also consume for a good purpose. Some people may say that everything is connected to each other and that we are all part of one holistic energy. Sure, if that works for you, but that does not help you train taking the external human perspective of a rock or a tree. And you need such flexible perspectives to be able to see every human as a human.
