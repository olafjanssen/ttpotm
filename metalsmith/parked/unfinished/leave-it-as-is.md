Following a path leaves footprints. Walking with a heavy step leaves deep footprints. Light-footed walk or floating leaves the path as you found it.

Leaving something as you found it symbolizes eternity, but one that takes effort. Everyone wants to make a lasting impression for eternity. But how do you know if your impression has been a good one of will be. Leaving things as you found it is an activity not a passivity en de preserving leaving shows a healing force, it symbolizes the battle against destruction and decay. It is the base to make a positive turn.

And what if you encounter chaos on your path? Then you uncover the true nature and enjoy the appearing beauty, even if you are performing someone else's job.
