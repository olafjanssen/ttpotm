The journey to the internal center tis one hat is untouchable, invisible and unattainable. It attracts and repulses. If the center becomes visible, tangible and attainable then you can be certain it is not the true center. That is to say, the center is never a thing onto itself but a linguistic or mathematical construction.

A few meaningul examples:
* your eye's pupil - lets light through
* eye of the storm - silence in a turbulent world
* atol - island with mountain, without the mountain
