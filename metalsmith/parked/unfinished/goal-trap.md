At one point, humanity will realise that there is no purpose to strive for, because the universe is without preordained purpose. Sadly, it is near impossible to rid yourself of a goal or sense of purpose. Whenever there is a striving or an ambition, there is a goal even if the goal is not to strive for a goal. If there is no goal, there can be no striving.

You will only not strive for something if you are not aware of the goal. Only if you are unaware of the lack of existence of a goal can you not strive for it. However, you will then strive for a non-existing goal because you are indeed aware of that. Being conscious create a striving, more awareness means more options for striving.

Our increasing awareness creates a barrier between us and the real pointlessness of our existence. We walk ahead and see there everything as a search for truth, while the truth lies always behind us, we can therefore only be aware of what the absolute truth is not.

The pain is in the realisation that every step takes you further away but you cannot go back. You cannot escape the setting and striving for a goal but you can find paths that go forward, because paths never go backward (in time except through auto time-travel).
