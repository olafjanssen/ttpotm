Our souls roam the universe, tunneling the multi-dimensional space-time continuum. Riding the waves of interpersonal energies repulsed and attracted to energy sinks and sources. Once in a while you are likely to be pulled into a strong vortex of infinite depth and solitude. This abyss of loneliness has a pull that onece you succumb there is going back.

The vortex isolats you from the outside world being a singularity in the fabric of the universe. The lack of connection is palpable with every sensing organ.

![loneliness-condition](/img/topic-loneliness.png)

Like standing in an echoless chamber (dead room) and everything returns into yourself, so sings the pain of loneliness around in your body. It seems as if your body collapses into itself to dive into the infinite depthtitude of the black hole of loneliness.

There is no connection so you cannot get out using someone else. Because the vortex runs so deep, when you ride the downward spiral with great velocity you end up so deep that you loose energy in the form of existential rage.

If that expresses itself in a uncontrollable balling session then that is a safe way; be sure not to express it in an unsafe way or let yourself be in a position to express it unsafely.

At this depth of the singularity there is an increasingly larger chance to tunnel out of the vortex, usually after a consciousless moment (sleep) en end up in calmer waters.

The transition that occurs during this tunneling in the subconscious also can me made consiously. There is a way of get out of the downward forcing pull.

Enjoy the ride. Be proud on your loneliness, on the fact that nobody understands you or feels you. You do not need any acknowledgment or recognizion when you are lonely. The vortex is real, the pain is the effort it takes in trying to escape it, even though it is pointless. That wasted energy prevents getting out alive.

Let yourself be taken to the bottom of the pit so that it is simpler and surer that you tunnel out in one piece.
