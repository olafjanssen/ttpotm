About the author

Olaf T.A. Janssen is a Dutch educator, storyteller, with a PhD in computational optical nanophysics, and a disloyal Auditor Apparent of the Collège de ’Pataphysique—because sometimes the best way to explore reality is to question it. For over a decade, he has taught computer science and coached flurries of students. He blends analytical rigor with a passion for game design, dabbling in both real and invented languages.

When he’s not writing, you might find him bumping doorframes as a left-handed, red-green color-blind, 2 meters tall giant, or pondering the quirks of aphantasia and AuDHD. His stories, like his mind, often stray from the expected—embracing the wild, the flawed, and the wonderfully human.
