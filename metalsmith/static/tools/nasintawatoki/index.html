This is a playful and modestly addictive toki pona learning app. It cleverly picks sentences from the tatoeba.org database, based on your learning progress and your preferred learning strategy.

Learning strategies

Choose your own strategy, from slow but easy learning towards fast but harder learning.

If you disagree with a solution, report the sentence using the report button or improve the tatoeba.org database directly on their site.
