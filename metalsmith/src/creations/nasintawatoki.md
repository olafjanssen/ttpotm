There is no need to study the language `toki pona` in order to be confused by my fallible teachings. However, the well keeps bubbling and the casting `sitelen` are clear. Therefore, I created a Duolingo-like learning experience for `toki pona` based on sentences found in the [Tatoeba](https://tatoeba.org/eng) `kulupu`, called `ilo Nasintawatoki`. This will let you think about the most important words and expressions right from the start, such as: `mi lon e sina` = 'I made you aware of reality'.
