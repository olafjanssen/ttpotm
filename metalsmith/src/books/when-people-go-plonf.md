# When People Go Plonf

A novel that captures the instant when familiar frameworks of understanding dissolve, and from that collapse, new forms of meaning and connection become possible.

## About

*When People Go Plonf* is a work of fiction that explores the limits of communication, the breakdown of shared reality, and the unexpected moments of clarity that can emerge from confusion. It's part of the same omniverse as *The Toki Ponist on the Mountain* and *Piqued*, continuing the themes of language, perception, and the constructed nature of experience.
