# Piqued

A novel that delves into the nature of language, the fluidity of identity, and the constructed nature of reality. Part of the TTPotM Omniverse, it extends the exploration of consciousness and communication beyond the original Toki Pona framework.

## About

*Piqued* continues the investigation into how language shapes thought, but expands beyond Toki Pona to explore broader philosophical questions about perception, understanding, and the stories we tell ourselves.
