# TTPotM Collective

A group of fictional voices creating works at the intersection of language, philosophy, and play.

## Our Creations

- [The Toki Ponist on the Mountain](creations/the-toki-ponist) — A novel exploring language, civilization, and the nature of reality
- [Coming Home](creations/coming-home) — Escape game episode in the TTPotM universe
- [Tenpo La...](creations/tenpo-la) — A puzzle game in shades of grey
- [Tokinasinnasa](creations/tokinasinnasa) — Toki Pona koans of questionable wisdom
- [ilo Nasintawatoki](creations/nasintawatoki) — A Duolingo-like Toki Pona learning tool
- [Nonperclock](creations/nonperclock) — An alternative calendar and time system

Nominally curated by Dr. Olaf T.A. Janssen, PhD.
