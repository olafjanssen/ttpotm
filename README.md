# TTPotM - The Toki Ponist on the Mountain

A collective of voices from an omniverse. Their stories don't need to exist—but do anyway. From the footnotes of reality, we acknowledge: mountains are lonely, and gurus need company.

## Overview

TTPotM (The Toki Ponist on the Mountain) is a creative collective showcasing:

- **Books (lipu mute)** - Written works and publications
- **Games (ilo musi)** - Interactive experiences and playable creations
- **Creations (musi mute)** - Various artistic and experimental works
- **NonPerClock** - A personal non-linear clock system for alternative time tracking
