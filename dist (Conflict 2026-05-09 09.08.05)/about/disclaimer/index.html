Disclaimer, disclosure and self-critique

Following my own web manifesto, this page shows a general disclaimer, disclosure and self-critique.

Disclaimer

There is no guru and there is no mountain. If you feel genuinely confused or depressed, you may seek professional help, and then decide what is best for you.

There are a lot of references to Toki Pona on this site. Toki Pona is an actual minimalist artificial language unleashed to the world by Sonja Lang (tokipona.org). She constructed the language as part of her own personal journey. However, this site does not in any way reflect her views on this world nor does it claim to present an official Toki Pona philosophy.

This site does not use cookies (check it yourself) nor does it ask for personal information. I do track your visits using (self-hosted) Matomo, but the data is anonymized and only used to check which pages are visited. If you visit links to external sites you may of course be tracked there. For the same information in more legal terms read the official Privacy Policy. If you do find you are being tracked, please let me know, and I will try to fix it.

Disclosure

I am not paid by any commercial sponsor except for the lovely donors. This allows me to continue working on this universe. The site and games will be published and developed using the name of my studio Awoken Well, which is a one-man operation.

Self-Critique

The most important self-critique right now is that this page should be filled with more information and I will probably be a hypocrite and not follow up on my own thoughts and ideas. As far as I know, all of the texts are my own. I cannot exclude that through means of inspiration some wise guru words may be similar or perhaps even precisely that of other published thinkers. Certainly, some ideas will seem to resonate on the ideas of others.
