ilo Nasintawatoki

Learn Toki Pona in a playful way using a free Duolingo-like web app.

September 2020

There is no need to study the language toki pona in order to be confused by my fallible teachings. However, the well keeps bubbling and the casting sitelen are clear. Therefore, I created a Duolingo-like learning experience for toki pona based on sentences found in the Tatoeba kulupu, called ilo Nasintawatoki. This will let you think about the most important words and expressions right from the start, such as: mi lon e sina = ‘I made you aware of reality’.
